# Kiiara Demo Video Script (4 minutes)

## Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
- **Visual**: Show the Kiiara dashboard with financial overview
- **Audio**: "Hello, I'm Kiiara, your wise grandmother AI. I help organize your finances with care and security."
- **Action**: Navigate to the dashboard showing balance, income, expenses, and savings goals

## Voice Interaction Demo (0:30 - 1:30)
- **Visual**: Switch to Voice Assistant tab showing ADK web UI
- **Action**: Click microphone button, speak: "Kiiara, I need to add an expense of $85 for groceries today"
- **Audio**: Hear Kiiara's warm response: "Of course, dear. I've recorded that expense. Your grocery spending is looking good this month."
- **Visual**: Show tool execution in ADK logs, then return to dashboard to see new transaction

## Nostr Identity Demo (1:30 - 2:00)
- **Visual**: Navigate to Settings page
- **Action**: Click "Generate New Identity" button
- **Visual**: Show newly generated Nostr keys (npub/nsec)
- **Audio**: "Your decentralized Nostr identity ensures secure, private interactions. Your keys never leave your device."
- **Action**: Copy public key to clipboard

## Financial Features Demo (2:00 - 3:00)
- **Visual**: Return to Dashboard
- **Action**: Click "Add Transaction" button, fill form for $2600 salary deposit
- **Audio**: "Let me help you track your income. Regular salary deposits are the foundation of good budgeting."
- **Visual**: Show updated balance and income statistics
- **Action**: Click "Pay Bills" quick action, show upcoming bills list
- **Audio**: "I've reminded you about your upcoming rent and internet bills. Planning ahead prevents late fees."

## Investment & Backup Demo (3:00 - 3:45)
- **Visual**: Navigate to Settings → Financial Integrations
- **Action**: Show mock connections to Nubank, Revolut, Coinbase
- **Audio**: "I can connect to your financial accounts for a complete picture. For now, I'm showing demo connections."
- **Action**: Enable Google Drive Backup toggle
- **Audio**: "Your financial data is encrypted with your Nostr key and safely backed up to Google Drive."
- **Visual**: Click "Backup Now" button, show success message

## Conclusion (3:45 - 4:00)
- **Visual**: Return to dashboard, show comprehensive financial overview
- **Audio**: "Kiiara is your caring financial companion. Voice-first, secure, and always learning. Try it today!"
- **Text Overlay**: "Install as Chrome App • $9.99/month • Gemini Live Agent Challenge"

## Technical Notes for Recording
1. Use screen recording software (OBS, Loom, or QuickTime)
2. Ensure clear audio for voice interactions
3. Show real-time response without cuts in voice demo
4. Include architecture diagram as overlay in conclusion
5. Keep total length under 4 minutes
